Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2024

Your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy outlines how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our application.

1. Information We Collect

Personal Data

When you register or use our app, we may collect:

  • Name
  • Email address
  • Other details you provide voluntarily

Usage Data

We automatically collect:

  • Device type and operating system
  • App version
  • Usage patterns and interactions
  • Access timestamps

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To provide and maintain our service
  • To improve and personalize user experience
  • To communicate updates and changes
  • To provide customer support
  • To analyze usage patterns and trends
  • To send promotional content (with consent)

3. Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your data. However, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure. We strive to protect your personal information but cannot guarantee absolute security.

4. Face Data Collection and Usage

Face Data Collection

Our app may collect facial images and scan data when you use the facial analysis features. This includes:

  • Facial images captured through your device camera
  • Facial feature data for analysis purposes

Use of Face Data

Face data collected by our app is used exclusively for:

  • Providing the facial analysis functionality you request
  • Processing your facial images to deliver analysis results

Storage and Sharing

Face data is primarily stored locally on your device. When you use the facial analysis features:

  • Face data may be temporarily transmitted to OpenAI/ChatGPT for analysis purposes only
  • We do not permanently store your face data on our servers
  • Face data is not shared with any other third parties

Retention Period

Face data is retained as follows:

  • Data stored on your device remains until you uninstall the app
  • Data transmitted to OpenAI/ChatGPT for analysis is not permanently stored and is only used for the immediate processing of your request
  • We do not maintain any face data on our servers

Your Control

You maintain control over your face data by:

  • Being able to delete locally stored face data at any time by uninstall the application

5. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy periodically. Changes will be posted here with an updated date. Please review this policy regularly.
